# SF Transit Watch

Real-time San Francisco Muni arrivals, on your wrist.

SF Transit Watch is a native Apple Watch app that shows live bus and train arrivals for SFMTA stops anywhere in the 511.org coverage area. Pair it with the iPhone companion to manage your 511 API key in one place and keep favorites in sync.

## Features

- **Live arrivals** — real-time departure times sourced from the 511.org regional feed.
- **Nearby stops** — automatically surfaces stops around you using Apple Watch GPS.
- **Favorites** — one-tap access to the stops you check most often.
- **Siri shortcuts** — ask "when's the next 38?" without tapping.
- **Complications** — put your next favorite stop's arrival on your watch face.
- **iPhone companion** — set your 511 API key once on the phone; the watch picks it up automatically.

## Coverage

SF Transit Watch covers every transit stop served by the 511.org feed — all nine Bay Area counties: Alameda, Contra Costa, Marin, Napa, San Francisco, San Mateo, Santa Clara, Solano, and Sonoma.

## Getting started

1. Install SF Transit Watch from the App Store.
2. Open the iPhone app and enter your free 511.org API key (see the [support page](support.html) for how to get one).
3. Open the watch app — your nearby stops will appear automatically.

## Privacy

SF Transit Watch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your location is used only on-device to find nearby stops. Your 511.org API key is stored locally on your devices and synced between phone and watch via Apple's encrypted WatchConnectivity framework.
